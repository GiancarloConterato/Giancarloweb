Cats

Cats are cute and here are some facts!

Only 20% of Orange Cats are Female.
Cats have fewer taste buds than dogs or people.
Cats don't get cavities.
Many Cats get the Zoomies after using the litter box.
Cat Whiskers are as Sensitive as Human Fingertips.
Cats are Nearsighted, but see great in the dark.

Why cats make good pets

Cats offer companionship, unconditional love, entertainment, affection, lots of purring, comfortable silences and they're trainable!
